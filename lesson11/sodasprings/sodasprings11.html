Soda Springs: Why soda?

By: Katie Perry

Soda Springs sounds like quite the interesting name for one of the oldest towns in Idaho. It acutally was one of the few towns with hot springs discovered on the Oregon Trail. The town received its name from the naturally carbonated water that occurs as result of the releasing of Geothermal gases. Thus the town's motto of "The Soda is on us". Literally, they town spews soda.
Did you know that their iconic Soda Spring geiser was actually the result of a well-drilled in an attempt to create a hot springs spa. Now they geiser is regulated by man made means. Interesting now isn't it? (references: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soda_Springs_Geyser)
